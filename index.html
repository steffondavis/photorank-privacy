Photo Rank for iOS

Photo Rank Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Photo Rank is designed to rank photos privately on your device. The app does not collect personal information or user data.

Overview

Photo Rank helps you compare and rank photos that you choose from your own photo library. No account or login is required, and Photo Rank does not collect, sell, share, or use personal information for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information We Collect

Photo Rank does not collect personal information or user data. The app does not collect analytics, diagnostics, usage data, device identifiers, contact information, location data, or any other information about you.

Photo Library Access

Photo Rank only accesses photos that you select or authorize through iOS photo library permissions. You remain in control of which photos are available to the app.

Local Processing and Storage

Photos are processed, compared, and ranked locally on your device. Photos and ranking data are stored locally on your device, and Photo Rank does not upload photos or ranking data to external servers.

Sharing

Shared content leaves Photo Rank only when you explicitly use the standard iOS share sheet and select a destination. Any information shared after that point is handled by the destination app or service you choose.

Third-Party Services

Photo Rank does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party data-collection services. The app does not use advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, tracking technologies, cookies, or tracking pixels.

Data Retention and Deletion

Ranking data remains locally on your device. You can delete ranking data by deleting sets within the app. You can remove all locally stored app data by deleting Photo Rank from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Photo Rank does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app does not collect user data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, contact support-app@curalytics.com.
